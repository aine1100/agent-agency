# Marketing Plan for Student Task Todo App

## Objective
Launch a user-friendly todo app designed specifically for students to manage their tasks efficiently. 

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Students (high school and college)
- **Secondary**: Parents of students, educators

## Key Features to Highlight
- Simple and intuitive task management.
- Local storage support for task persistence.
- Ability to add/delete tasks easily.
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop use.

## Marketing Strategies
1. **Digital Advertising**: 
   - Google Ads targeting students searching for task management tools.
   - Facebook and Instagram ads with engaging visuals.

2. **Content Marketing**: 
   - Blog posts on effective study techniques and time management tips for students.
   - Tutorials on how to use the app and maximize productivity.

3. **Social Media Campaigns**: 
   - Create eye-catching posts with user testimonials.
   - Run a "Task Challenge" on platforms like TikTok or Instagram stories encouraging students to share their task lists.

4. **Influencer Collaborations**: 
   - Partner with student influencers or educational content creators for promotions and reviews.

5. **Launch Promotions**: 
   - Offer a limited-time "share with a friend" discount or referral program.

## Success Metrics
- Achieve a user retention rate of 50% within the first three months.
- Reach 1,000 active users within the first six months.
- Generate at least 500 social media shares of user testimonials within the first month.

## Timeline
- **Month 1**: Launch website, social media presence, and initial ads.
- **Month 2**: Begin content marketing and influencer outreach.
- **Month 3**: Evaluate campaign effectiveness and adjust strategies as necessary.
