## 2. Risk Checklist

- **Technical Risks:**
  - Compatibility issues across different browsers.
  - Performance concerns with local storage as the app scales.
  
- **Design Risks:**
  - Difficulty in achieving consistent styling across devices.
  - Failure to meet accessibility standards.

- **User Experience Risks:**
  - Users struggle with the interface.
  - Missing features leading to user dissatisfaction.

- **Project Management Risks:**
  - Timeline overruns due to scope changes.
  - Lack of clear communication among team members.

## 4. Success Criteria for QA

- **Functionality:**
  - Users can add, delete, and edit todos without errors.
  - Completed todos can be toggled on and off.

- **Performance:**
  - The app loads in under 2 seconds on a standard connection.
  - Local storage correctly persists data after page refresh.

- **Accessibility:**
  - Achieve a WCAG AA rating (color contrast, keyboard navigation).
  - All form elements are labeled correctly for screen readers.

- **Usability:**
  - Users can successfully navigate and use the app without guidance.
  - Conduct user testing, and at least 80% of testers provide positive feedback.

- **Compatibility:**
  - The app functions correctly on major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
