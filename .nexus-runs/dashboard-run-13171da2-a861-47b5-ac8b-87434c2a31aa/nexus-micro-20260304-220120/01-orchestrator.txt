### NEXUS-Micro Mode Activated

#### Execution Plan for Landing Page Development

**Phase 1: Project Analysis & Planning**
1. **Objective and Requirements Gathering**
   - Understand the purpose and target audience of the landing page.
   - Define the key elements to include: services offered, client testimonials, contact form, call to action.

2. **Specification Document**
   - Draft a specification based on the analysis detailing layout, color scheme, typography, and functionality.

---

**Phase 2: Design & UX**
1. **Initial Design Mockups**
   - Create wireframes and design mockups based on the specification.
2. **Feedback Loop**
   - Gather feedback from stakeholders and revise designs accordingly.

---

**Phase 3: Development**
1. **Frontend Development**
   - Implement the landing page using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, adhering to provided designs.
2. **Mobile Responsiveness**
   - Ensure the landing page is fully responsive across devices and screen sizes.
3. **SEO Optimization**
   - Optimize for search engines with appropriate metadata and alt tags.

---

**Phase 4: Quality Assurance**
1. **EvidenceQA Testing**
   - Conduct rigorous visual and functional testing to identify any issues.
   - Utilize screenshot evidence for validation.

---

**Phase 5: Launch & Monitoring**
1. **Final Review**
   - Review the final product with stakeholders for approval.
2. **Deployment**
   - Launch the landing page and monitor performance metrics.

---

### Risk Checklist

1. **Design Risks**
   - Incomplete or unclear design specifications leading to misalignment with stakeholder expectations.
  
2. **Development Risks**
   - Technology issues (browser compatibility, responsive design failures).
  
3. **Quality Assurance Risks**
   - Incomplete testing cycles failing to capture all issues.
  
4. **Timeline Risks**
   - Delays in feedback or approval processes.
  
5. **SEO Risks**
   - Missing SEO best practices leading to low visibility.

---

### Handoff Instructions for Specialist Agents

1. **Project-Manager-Senior**
   - Handoff the finalized project specification to ensure all tasks align with the document before proceeding.

2. **UI Designer**
   - Provide clear instructions on the desired aesthetic and functional components. Include reference links or documents showcasing desirable landing page designs.

3. **Frontend Developer**
   - Deliver comprehensive mockups from the UI Designer, along with the specification document detailing layout, fonts, and interactive elements. Emphasize the importance of mobile responsiveness and SEO best practices.

4. **EvidenceQA Agent**
   - Supply all relevant implementation details and criteria for validation. Include a checklist of features to test and specify requirements for screenshots during the QA process.

---

### Success Criteria for QA

1. **Visual Consistency**
   - The landing page must match the approved design mockups.
  
2. **Functional Completeness**
   - All elements (forms, buttons, links) must function seamlessly without errors.

3. **Responsiveness**
   - The landing page must display correctly across various devices (mobile, tablet, desktop).

4. **Performance Metrics**
   - Page load time must not exceed 3 seconds for optimal user experience.

5. **SEO Compliance**
   - The page must adhere to SEO standards, including proper tags, descriptions, and alt texts for images.

---

With this structured approach in place, the development of the landing page can proceed efficiently, ensuring quality and alignment with stakeholder expectations at every phase.
