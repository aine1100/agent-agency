# Marketing Plan for Restaurant Management App

## Objective
To promote the Restaurant Management App, targeting restaurant owners and managers looking for a task management solution.

## Target Audience
- Restaurant Owners
- Restaurant Managers
- Small Business Entrepreneurs
- Hospitality Management Professionals

## Key Features to Highlight
- Task addition and management
- Persistent task storage using localStorage
- Simple and user-friendly interface
- Potential for future enhancements (editing, notifications)

## Channels for Promotion
- **Website**: Create a dedicated landing page
- **Social Media**: Engage audience via Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn
- **Email Marketing**: Distribute newsletters highlighting app features
- **Online Communities**: Participate in forums related to restaurant management

## Launch Timeline
1. **Week 1**: Finalize app features and marketing materials
2. **Week 2**: Launch website and social media campaigns
3. **Week 3**: Begin email marketing and community engagement

## Metrics to Measure Success
- Website traffic and engagement
- Social media engagement rates
- Email open and conversion rates
- User feedback and app downloads
