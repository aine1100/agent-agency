# Marketing Plan for Restaurant Management Todo App

## Objective
To launch a React JS application targeting restaurant managers and staff, helping them streamline task management and enhance operational efficiency.

## Target Audience
- Restaurant managers
- Kitchen staff
- Front of house staff
- Restaurant owners

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
A user-friendly task management app designed specifically for restaurants, allowing team collaboration, task tracking, and operational efficiency.

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Logo**: Modern design reflecting culinary themes
- **Color Scheme**: Soft earthy tones combined with vibrant colors to reflect freshness
- **Typography**: Clean, sans-serif typefaces for readability

## Key Features
1. Task management with categories specific to restaurant needs
2. User authentication for multi-role access (manager, staff)
3. Notifications for deadlines and task assignments
4. Responsive design for mobile and tablet users
5. Integration with POS systems

## Marketing Channels
- **Social Media**: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn for targeted ads
- **Content Marketing**: Blog posts on best practices in restaurant management
- **Email Marketing**: Targeted campaigns to restaurant associations and industry professionals
- **SEO**: Optimize for keywords like "restaurant task management" and "restaurant operations software"

## Budget Allocation
- Social Media Ads: 30%
- Content Creation: 25%
- Email Campaigns: 20%
- SEO Tools: 15%
- Design & Branding: 10%

## Timeline
- Week 1: Finalize branding and create social content
- Week 2: Launch website landing page and begin social media ads
- Week 3: Start email campaign and release blog posts
- Week 4: Gather feedback and adjust strategy

## Success Metrics
- Increase sign-ups by 50% within the first month
- Achieve a minimum of 20% engagement rate on social media
- Gather at least 100 email subscribers by the end of month one
